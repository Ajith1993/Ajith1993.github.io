So How it all started.......  

After completing my school, I didn't know what I was going to do. Finally , I got into a Bachelor Science degree in Electronics.
Upon completing my degree, I thought I should do know the business end. Maybe I had a career there.So I enrolled for a PGDM programme specializing in Systems and Operations at SCMS Cochin School of Business.

During the PGDM programme itself , I got a job at Somany Ceramics Ltd. I worked there for a year as a Management Trainee - Sales.

While working, I realised that eventhough I excelled in completing my targets, I didn't find the satisfaction.

I have a habit of being in touch with my batchmates. From one of my friends , I had the opportunity to know about Data Analytics. I was extremely interested in the domain and I enrolled for an online course at Datacamp to know more about the field. I found that I enjoyed the course and understood it pretty well.

So, I took the decision to quit my job and fully concentrate on data science.


Cutback to present day!!!

Today , I have a pretty good knowledge about the analytics field and I am confident that I can tackle any problem that's been thrown my way....
